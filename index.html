Project Reflections

As Gatsby gazed at the green light, so too do we chase the promise of perfect code. Here are the lessons, triumphs, and foibles from our journey.

Pros

  • Debugging is very fast.
  • AI handles the drudgery allowing me to focus on building the quality.
  • Testing is thorough, but never dull.

Cons

  • AI can't interpret visual data, such as how things are rendered in a browser.
  • Some mysteries require a human touch.
  • Cursor still has limited capabilities, needing a skilled professional guiding it to meet requirements.

Observations

  • The pair coding was very succesful very quickly
  • We still encountered the typical speedbumps in development, but were able to overcome them quickly
  • Cursor AI is a fine companion, but the adventure is yours.
